Visualisation and Insights from Rural Health Statistics 2016¶

As on 31st March, 2015, there were 153655 Sub Centres, 25308 Primary Health Centres (PHCs) and 5396 Community Health Centres (CHCs) functioning in the country. While the Sub Centres, PHCs and CHCs have increased in number in 2014-15, the current numbers are not sufficient to meet their population norm.
